# Annotated Bibliography: Breakfast and Cardiovascular Health

This document provides a curated collection of research papers examining the relationship between breakfast consumption habits and cardiovascular health markers. Each entry includes a full citation, summary of the study's objective, methods, key findings, and a critique or reflection on its relevance and limitations.

The goal is to build a living, version-controlled resource for researchers, clinicians, and health enthusiasts interested in the impact of dietary patterns on heart health.

---

*Entries are listed in reverse chronological order (most recent first).*
